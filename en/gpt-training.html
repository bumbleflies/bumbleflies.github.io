Your Path to GPT Mastery

Mastering ChatGPT

Book Course

Welcome to the "Mastering ChatGPT" Workshop Series!

In an era where artificial intelligence (AI) and chatbots are increasingly important, we offer you a tailored workshop series that introduces you to the world of smart chat systems. Whether you are a creative mind, a product expert, or an experienced moderator, our courses are specially designed to equip you with the tools to effectively and creatively use ChatGPT.

Basic Course: “Introduction to ChatGPT”

Start your journey with our Basic Course, specially designed for playful and creative people. In this introductory course, you will learn how to use the basic version of ChatGPT for creating text content like dialogues, stories, or creative writing exercises. It’s the ideal way to explore the basics and versatility of ChatGPT in an entertaining manner.

Plus Course: “Advanced Capabilities with ChatGPT Plus”

Dive deeper into the world of ChatGPT Plus and discover how you can create not only texts but also impressive visual content with this enhanced version. In the Plus Course, you will learn how to use ChatGPT Plus for developing visually appealing projects such as illustrated stories or creative image compositions. Experience how this advanced technology expands your creative possibilities.

Product Course: “Successful Product Launch with ChatGPT Plus”

This course is for all product managers and marketing experts who want to learn how to use ChatGPT in product development and marketing. From market research to logo development – this course covers all aspects important for a successful product launch.

Moderators Course: “Workshop Design and Execution with ChatGPT Plus”

For moderators and trainers who want to expand their skills, this course is just right. Learn how to plan, conduct, and evaluate effective and interactive workshops using ChatGPT.

Get ready to master ChatGPT! Whether you want to expand your creative skills, market your product more effectively, or improve your moderation techniques – this workshop series provides you with the tools and knowledge you need to be successful in the world of chatbots and AI.

The course language for all courses is German
