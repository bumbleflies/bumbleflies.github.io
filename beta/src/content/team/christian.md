## Background

Christian brings over 15 years of experience in organizational development and facilitation. As an organizational psychologist, he has worked with organizations across industries to unlock authentic dialogue and drive meaningful transformation. His background spans corporate strategy, nonprofit leadership development, and deep expertise in creating spaces where teams can think together.
